Brev is a social app for discovering and sharing nightlife. Post photos of where you're drinking, tag venues, and see what's happening around you. Think of it as Instagram Stories meets a venue discovery app.

Tap the camera button in the bottom navigation bar, then:

  1. Take a photo or choose one from your gallery
  2. Add a caption (optional, up to 200 characters)
  3. Tag a venue (optional)
  4. Tap the send button to post

Your post will appear in the feed immediately.

Yes! You can browse as a guest to view posts and explore venues. However, you'll need to create an account to post, like, or comment.

There are two ways to edit your profile:

  • Go to your Profile tab and tap the "Edit Profile" button
  • Or tap the gear icon ⚙️ on your profile, then select "Edit Profile"

You can update your name, username, and bio. Avatar uploads coming soon!

While viewing a post, tap the heart ❤️ to like it. Tap again to unlike. Tap the comment 💬 button to view and add comments. You must be signed in to like or comment.

Post and comment deletion is coming in the next update. For now, if you need something removed, email us at help@brev.app and we'll take care of it within 24 hours.

Password reset is handled by our authentication provider. Email help@brev.app with your account email and we'll send you a reset link within 24 hours.

Content that violates our Terms of Service may be removed. This includes nudity, hate speech, harassment, illegal content, and spam. If you believe your content was removed in error, contact help@brev.app.

Brev is available worldwide! As long as you have an internet connection, you can use Brev. The app is currently in English. Additional language support may come in the future.

We collect minimal data to make Brev work — your email, username, posts, and basic usage data. We never sell your data or track your precise location. See our full Privacy Policy for details.

If you encounter harassment, inappropriate content, or technical issues, email help@brev.app. Please include screenshots if possible. We take all reports seriously and respond within 48 hours.
