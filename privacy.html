Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

TL;DR: We collect minimal data to make Brev work. We never sell your personal information. Your photos are stored securely on Cloudinary. You can delete your account and data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Brev, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your email address, username, full name, and a hashed version of your password (we never see your actual password).
  • Content You Create: Photos you upload, captions you write, venue tags, and comments you post.
  • Usage Data: We collect basic analytics including which features you use, app crashes, and performance data to improve Brev.
  • Device Information: Your device type, operating system version, and app version. We do NOT track your precise location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your posts, comments, and profile to other users
  • Send you essential account-related emails (password reset, email verification)
  • Improve Brev's functionality and fix bugs
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your photos for advertising without permission
  • Track your location in the background
  • Send marketing emails without your consent

3. Data Storage & Security

Authentication: Your account credentials are managed by Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS for data in transit.

Photos: Images you upload are stored on Cloudinary, a secure cloud media platform. Photos are publicly accessible via URL but only displayed within the Brev app.

Database: Post data, comments, and profile information are stored in Supabase's managed PostgreSQL database with row-level security enabled.

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

4. Third-Party Services

Brev uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Supabase — Authentication, database, and API services. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudinary — Image hosting and delivery. Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — Typography. Privacy Policy
  • Font Awesome — Icons via CDN. Privacy Policy

These services may collect their own usage data according to their respective privacy policies.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

Brev uses your browser's local storage to keep you signed in between visits. We store your authentication token locally on your device. This token is automatically deleted when you log out.

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. Brev does not display advertisements.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@brev.app. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Brev is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a child under 13 has used Brev, please contact us at privacy@brev.app.

8. International Users

Brev is operated from South Africa. If you access Brev from outside South Africa, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence. By using Brev, you consent to such transfers.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile information is immediately deleted
  • Your posts and comments are immediately deleted
  • Uploaded photos may remain in Cloudinary's cache for up to 24 hours
  • Backup copies may persist for up to 30 days

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or through the app before the changes take effect. Continued use of Brev after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at:

📧 privacy@brev.app

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
